IT-415-DL1 Fall 2017 Final Presentation by Nicholas Richardson

This Tableau Visualization explores the IUCN's Threatened Species List. The data provided gives the number of threatened species by major groups of organisms from years 2000-2017.

For this visualization story, I chose the major categories Vertebrates, Invertebrates, Plants, and Fungi + Protists.

I then made multiple visualizations for the Tableau Story. The following visualizations were made for the story:

  1. Vertebrates' species using a Line Chart
  2. Invertebrates' species using an Area Chart
  3. Plants using an Area Chart
  4. Fungi & Protists using a Tree Map
  5. 2017 Total Number of Species Threatened by Major Organism Group
The reason I decided to include this final visualization was because since I am telling a story, I wanted to conclude my micro-level graphs for each major organism group with a macro-scale graph to help the audience visualize the endangered species list in the current day.

I hope you enjoy my final visualization for IT 415!
